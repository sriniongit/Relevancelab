Life Sciences Solutions – Focus on removing inefficiencies and optimizing operations in a Clinical Trial Network

The Life Sciences Industry has a huge number of players dealing with Large Pharma, Specialized Biotech’s, Clinical Research Organizations (large global players and small regional players), Clinical Sites, Investigators, Regulatory authorities and large population of patients. This large scale operation with unique needs for documents handling, regulatory compliances, data privacy, and high growth in emerging markets with poor infrastructure, puts unique challenges on the Integrated Platform needs for this market. Any typical Large Trial (especially in Late Phase) involves Sponsors managing trials across multiple countries with multi-lingual needs, number of CRO/SMO players, number of Clinical Sites/Investigators and large number of subjects. Such large scale network of players has different business, functional and system needs that cannot be catered by any one system and requires a new approach to solving this problem.

Opportunity

There is a disruption happening in this industry as market pressures and lack on new block-buster drugs are forcing companies to look at new avenues to increase innovation, focus on outcomes, adopt new technologies and focus on data analytics for more efficient operations. The consolidation of existing Product players by larger companies has created a vacuum in the segment for small and mid-sized companies. Even products of large technology players are based on older platforms and not integrated – there is a need to create a next generation platform leveraging new concepts like Unified Experience Portals (Integrating Document Management, Workflows and Data Analytics), support multiple models of deployment (Cloud, On-premise, Hybrid etc.) and enable of multi-channel access with mobile devices etc.

  • Central and Site CTMS integrated information flow
  • Clinical Operations Governance Portal for Data Management and Pharmacovigilance
  • Light Weight EDC, IVR and Safety Solutions
  • Document and Workflow Management
  • Operational and Clinical Data Warehouse
  • SDTM Mapping Tools and Utilities
  • Clinical Network Cloud Integration Messaging Framework
  • Clinical Data Analysis and Dashboards
  • Unique Patient Identification Systems using Bio-metrics
