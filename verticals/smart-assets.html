Smart Assets Creation

"Relevance Lab specializes in creating Smart Assets in the form of products and platforms by leveraging the new technologies in Cloud, Mobility and Big Data. As new products get conceptualized the paradigm has shifted from products being shipped in CDs to a hosted SAAS based offerings and this drives a new generation of Enterprise Architecture covering Engineering and Operations integrated with DevOps philosophy. The growth in these new areas is coming from start-ups and large enterprises creating a huge market potential emerging in the space of IP creation and new product developments on Cloud platforms."

DevOps Driven Smart Assets Creation

With existing investments in creating a DevOps platform, Relevance Lab is offering the ability to tap DevOps with the same ease of utility models in an On-demand manner. Our Cloud based platform enables collaboration, agile development, release engineering and deployments automation for creation of Smart Assets efficientlythat can save many months of internal efforts and provide a model to leverage the best practices from multiple implementation experiences.

Product development has a different mind-set compared to traditional projects since the focus is on creating a “Technology Foundation” that is scalable and changes with changing business needs. The paradigm of a product is for multi-customer solutions than of custom project solutions. This drives a different approach on Requirements Management with Product Management concept, Architecture based on Re-usable Platforms, Engineering Rigor with emphasis on tools and automation and Focus on Production Deployments, Performance, Systems Management and End-User Experience focus – a core philosophy made available with Technology, Tools and Best Practices delivered through the DevOps Platform.

Building Technology Incubation Centres
