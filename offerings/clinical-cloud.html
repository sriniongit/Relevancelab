Clinical Trial Platform

Clinical Trial Platform (CTP) manages the core part of clinical trial life-cycle. The CTP solution is built to support both On-premise and SAAS based deployments. It is based on an UxP (Unified Experience Portal) and provides an integrated solution for workflows, documents and data analytics. It is part of our Clinical Cloud solution built for interactions between the Clinical Network of Sponsors, CROs, Sites, Investigators and Patients. The CTP is built as framework of loosely coupled components that can be easily customized to support different business processes and data elements – also integrate with existing systems of customers. Along with our Clinical Operations Governance Portal it provides a complete solution for managing multiple trials, monitoring data and operations with integration to a Trial Data repository for reporting and dashboards.

Site Trial Platform

Site Trial Management solution automates the investigator tasks to manage site workflows, tasks and operation. One of the key areas of inefficiencies in Clinical Trial process is due to lack of Site Automation that leads to data quality issues at source and lack of real time visibility. Sites have unique challenges and need a light weight CTMS system preferably in Cloud/SAAS model that is very easy to use by site coordinators and investigators.

Clinical Portal

Existing organizations have implemented critical operational systems using silo functional solutions that have failed to provide integrated workflows, monitoring dashboards, decision support, reviews and action management. The pressure to increase operational efficiencies in the Clinical Trial value chain is pushing the needs for a strong Clinical Operations Governance Portal (COGP). For managing clinical trials Pharma companies typically use two dominant engagement models with CROs

  • Full Service Model
  • Specialized Functional Services Model

In either case the operations deal with a large number of CROs, Clinical Sites, Clinical Labs and Investigators and their fragmented systems. Different functional activities need different metrics and monitoring SLAs in Data Management, Pharmacovigilance, Biostatistics, Medical Writing, Project and Site management, Resource and Financials tracking. Many times there are different systems on EDC, Safety, IVRS, Project/Portfolio Planning, CTP, CDMS, LIMS, manual processes etc. The COGP provides a Unified Experience Platform (UxP) for planning, data consolidation, collaboration, information delivery and analytics to Pharma companies and Clinical Research Organizations. It is integrated with data feeds of multiple systems and provides people operational and management reports and dashboards.

Clinical Operations Governance Portal COGP is a role based operations governance system that not only provides you real-time dashboards but also offers alerts to manage SLAs and risks.

eTMF

eTMF (Electronic Trial Master File) ensures the critical trial documents are in synch and central managers can review documents real time. There are a number of scenarios for Documents and Tasks to be assigned across the network of players from sponsors, CROs to sites and investigators. On a day to day basis a number of documents, queries and e-mails are exchanged leading to in-efficient tracking of right document versions, timely responses and data updates. All these workflows and documents are streamlined using a hosted solution that connects different players interacting in a Clinical Trial.

Clinical Monitoring

Clinical Monitoring helps trial manager’s plan, track risks using multi source data. Based on FDA recommended Risk Based Clinical Monitoring this solution helps trial managers identify, mitigate, avoid site and subject risks for each trial /activity. This solution can be easily integrated with existing systems that may exist like EDC, CTMS, IVRS, Safety, eTMF etc. and provide a very effective planning, tracking and decision making tool for Clinical Trials monitoring.

Clinical Data Repository

One of the critical areas to address in Clinical trials deals with data acquisition, cleansing, master data management, CDISC compliances, data storage, analysis, reporting and submissions. Clinical Data Repository is built using CDISC standards and serves as a common repository for Operational and Clinical data collected from downstream systems and used for integrated analytics and dashboards.
