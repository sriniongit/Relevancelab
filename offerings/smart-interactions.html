Smart Interactions

"Smart Interactions are about combining the power of Technology, Data and Customer Interactions in today’s digital world to provide scalable, high performance and relevant information. Relevance Lab along with a specialist partner has created two solutions to address the needs of this segment. One solution leverages the power of Social Media Data to address Customer and Brand Insights for Key Verticals based on Social Media Data analysis, historical data processing and a smart recommendations engine. The other solution deals with a high performance and real-time information processing to provide a “context aware” experience."

Smart Social Interactions - Big Data Based Domain Specific Brand and Sentiment Analysis Platform

While a number of solutions exist for Social data monitoring for Brands, organizations are taking this to next level to monitor Brand Insights, Sentiment Analysis and integrate the data for actionable intelligence. Our platform can be embedded inside an enterprise and integrated with your existing CRM and Marketing solutions to provide deeper insights. Common use cases for leveraging such a platform are:

  • Meaningful social insights
    • Identifying consumer pain points
    • Product satisfaction index
    • Product suggestions/ideas from social communications
  • Social CRM ( Ticket creation in CRM )
  • Social Marketing Impact Index
  • Brand Sentiments & Engagement patterns
  • Lead Generation from Social Messages

The solution is built to go beyond the standard common Social Media sites (Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr etc.) and easily integrate with Vertical specific sites for Acquiring, Storing and Analysing the data from online channels. The key power of the platform comes from Domain specific crawlers that make it easy to scale for different domains and sources with minimal effort.

Cloud Data Solutions

For most enterprises there is a critical need to leverage their Data Assets and extract more information and analytics that drive their business decisions. Relevance Lab is working with large enterprises in key verticals to explore the right use cases of emerging Big Data solutions leveraging Cloud Platforms.
